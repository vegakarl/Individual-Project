Bloodborne


Are you looking for a challenge? A game with steampunk and gothic atmosphere? Eldritch Horrors? Then if yes, this game has all of those. The game’s story is centered about blood, hence the title BLOODborne. This is my entry point to the soulsborne games which made me fall in love with the genre and games with very deep lore. The music is fantastic, story writing is also very good, and the game is very unforgiving especially with each boss serving a different challenge.

My favorite part of this game is its weapons wherein every weapon has an alternate form which made pulling out a lot of combos possible with different builds that would also mean a lot of replayability for the game. The player would also find themselves overwhelmed with hoards of monsters starving for your blood, and different areas with breathtaking sceneries that are very relevant to the game’s lore.

I wouldn’t really recommend this game to those who are unfamiliar with the soulsborne franchise, and I would suggest that they should play something like Dark Souls or Demon’s Souls first before tackling this game.
