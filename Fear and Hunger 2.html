Fear and Hunger 2: Termina


What would you do when a peculiar creature tells you that you are now involved in a Battle Royale which requires you to be the only survivor among the passengers of the train that you are currently in. Fear and Hunger 2: Termina is like the better brother of Fear and Hunger wherein it took all of the concepts of the first installment and improved it by a huge margin. This installment is very unforgiving, heavily relies on skill, and luck, wherein all of the decisions of the player could become dire consequences which could end your entire playthrough.

This game piqued my interest regarding how all of the 14 participants of the Termina Festival have alternate forms which shows how they truly see themselves as an individual, which adds an entire depth of layer to these unique characters. These could be more understood by having us, the players analyze these alternate forms. I would rate this game as a solid 8/10 but if you are going to try this game, I would recommend you to be more open minded and be ready for the grotesque themes that this game features.
