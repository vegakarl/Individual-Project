Hades


If you’re looking for an action-packed game mixed with Greek mythology, then this game is for you. The story begins with Zagreus, the son of Hades, the god of the underworld. Zagreus seeks to escape from the underworld and reach Mt. Olympus, the home of his kind in order to defy his father’s orders and find more answers about who he truly is.

The game revolves around navigating through different areas of the underworld, and battling waves and waves of enemies with the help of powerful artifacts and, boons and blessings from your relatives. There’s a lot of replayability in this game as death is not the end. Each run is unique wherein you can interact with the gods and the citizens of the underworld and form bonds with them that will help you in your future runs.

My favorite part from this game is that there is a very low chance that the same dialogue would be given to you whenever you interact with a character which highlights the personalities of the cast of characters in this game, The music also hypes you up which is like a huge bonus for me as a lover of video game music. Hades II is also coming out in the near future which is I am very excited about. I’m rating this game a solid 10/10 as it is very fun and engaging, and could also sometimes be frustrating as it is not for the faint of heart.
