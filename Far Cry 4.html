Far Cry 4


The game starts with the main character, going to the place wherein he promised his mom to spread her ashes at, but he suddenly gets kidnapped and question by the military wherein the leader is a very corrupt and insane individual named Pagan Min.

The mechanics of this game is mostly the same as its predecessor, Far Cry 3 but with an entirely new story and set of villains. At first, I thought that this is just another version of this game, But, it really did surprise me as I had a lot of fun in the new things that this game brought to the series especially the hunting part as that was the part that I enjoyed the most as I would mostly go around and hunt different animals with my bow, in order to upgrade most of my equipment. If you loved Far Cry 3 then you would most definitely love this game too.
