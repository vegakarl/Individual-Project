Corpse Party


After performing a ritual for a friend that will transfer out to an entirely new city, 8 students and one teacher suddenly gets teleported into a broken down school with a very eerie vibe. The group gets separated and bad things starts to happen such as the presence of the ghosts of the students that haunts the halls of the school.

Corpse party is a top-down horror RPG with pixelated graphics that is probably one of the most popular game and anime back then for anime fans that are looking for a gorey, horror game that would definitely scare the wits out of you as it features a lot of gruesome themes such as dismemberment, suicide, betrayal, and murder. I would definitely recommend this game to people that are looking for something different.
