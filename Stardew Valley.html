Stardew Valley


Are you looking for a chill and relaxing game that has pixelated graphics? Then Stardew Valley is the answer to that. Stardew Valley starts when you inherit you late grandpa’s farm in a small town called, Stardew Valley wherein there is a whole cast of characters each with their own traits and stories that you can help them out with.

This game is mainly inspired by one of the classic farming games, Harvest Moon. Your main goal in this game is to start out a new life as a farmer and become a functional member of the community in this small town. This game will also take out all your stress as there is nothing urgent to be done here, you can always do things at your own pace. In my opinion, the developer of this game is probably one of the best people out there as he always gives out free updates that has a lot of content for free from time to time, and he also supports the moding community as it is very active and could give the game a breathe of new life.

So, if you just want to chill out alone or with friends, you should try out this game as it is very fun.
