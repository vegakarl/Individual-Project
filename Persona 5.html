Persona 5


You are now a leader of a vigilante group with a goal to expose the crimes of corrupt adults. Persona 5 is probably the game that has the best UI, Music, and cast of characters for me since it is very stylish and features jazz music that suits the atmosphere of the game. At first, I didn’t know anything about the Persona series and the only reason that I bought this game is that I liked the cover art which is probably one of the greatest decisions in life because of how this game opened my mind to JRPG games. The game references a lot of mythologies, and beliefs from different cultures and countries including Greek Mythology, the catholic bible, Japanese mythical creatures, Norse Mythology, and many more. Which could also be fun since it featured different takes from these ideas. I would rate this game a 9/10, the only reason it was not 10/10 is because of how annoying the side kick character, Morgana is. I would highly recommend this game for everyone to try.
