Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice


A lone wolf tasked to protect the divine heir of the Ashina Family that possesses the Dragons Blood that has a power related to immortality. Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is a soulslike game with ninjas, samurais, giant apes, and literal giants. Sekiro only allows the player to use a katana, but you can utilize a wide set of attachments to your prosthetic arm, and the main combat gimmick of this game is poise wherein after you break the poise of the enemy, you can perform a special move that can execute the enemy.

The storytelling style of Sekiro is very similar to the soulslike games wherein most of them are in the descriptions of the items. This game is as challenging as the other soulslike games as it is made by the same developer, the Fromsoft company. If you like the soulslike genre, you would definitelty love this game.
