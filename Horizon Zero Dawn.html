Horizon Zero Dawn


This game features the dystopian future ravaged because of the surge of machines called the Faro Plague. Horizon Zero Dawn offers an interesting take on the open-world genre wherein the world is recovering from an apocalypse brought by machines that are inspired by wildlife has their own quirks and weaknesses that the player needs to hunt in order to upgrade their equipment, some of these are docile while most of them will hunt the player on sight.

The game features a lush environment wherein you need to fight of a cult with the goal to re-eradicate life in the world again by bringing back the Faro Plague. Horizon Zero Dawn also has the radio towers in the form of climbing these very large machines that is possibly inspired by giraffes. The player is required to scale these large machines in order to unlock a portion of the map for further exploration, which is my favorite part of the game as it made me discover a lot of breath-taking sights in the game. I would rate this game a solid 8/10.
