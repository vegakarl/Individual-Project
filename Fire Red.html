Pokemon Fire Red


This game introduced me to the realm of gaming and one of the games that I go back to from time to time whenever I feel like I want to reminisce the past. Pokemon Fire Red is a top down RPG with pixelated graphics wherein you have to catch creatures using Pokeballs that you can train to evolve into a stronger version.

The way combat works in this game is that it’s turn-based and each pokemon has different typings that are stronger against certain types and weaker against certain types. The game progresses through beating gym leaders which allows the player to move on to the next area. The music for each area is great wherein you won’t get sick of them even if you are listening to them repeatedly.

All in All, I’d suggest everyone to play this game as it’s not a challenging game, you just have to grind and strengthen your pokemon in order to defeat the enemies.
