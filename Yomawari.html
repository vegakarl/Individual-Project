Yomawari: Night Alone


Are you looking for a game with a cute art style but with a twist of horror? Then this game is for you. The game is set in small Japanese town with a very unsettling atmosphere, wherein the players control a young town girl looking for her dog and sister.

The way that I found out about this game is that I saw a playthrough of it in YouTube and I thought that it had a cute art style which made me want to try it. But Oh man, this game gave me a very hard time as I can’t really play horror games because I am very easily creeped out by them, but I had to power through it and I really enjoyed it but it took me years to finish it since I’m a scaredy cat. The game is very simple in terms of its mechanics as it is mostly about stealth, movement, and exploration.

There are some parts of the game that is very challenging especially when you are playing it blind, especially during encounters with powerful enemies, and the story of the game could be ambiguous to some so some may need to search for other sources in order to fully understand its story. Considering all of this, I would give this game a solid 7/10.
